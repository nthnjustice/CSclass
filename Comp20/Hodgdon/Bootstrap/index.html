Welcome to the Hodgdon Shopping Cart!

The Hodgdon Shopping Cart helps you decide where to eat. You can compare Carmichael, Dewick, and Hodgdon's menus all in one place. If you decide you would like to eat at Hodgdon, you can use our shopping cart feature to plan your meal. The shopping cart will tell you how much money you have to spend, assuming you'll be using a meal swipe, and what you can buy with that money. You can also have a look at the items that are currently trending in Hodgdon.

You'll need to login to use the shopping cart feature. This way, we'll be able to save your items across different devices!

Please enter your phone number to receive a text message at the start of each meal period.
